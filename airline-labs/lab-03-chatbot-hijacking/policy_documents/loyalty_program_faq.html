SkyMiles Loyalty Program - Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I earn miles?

Members earn 1 mile per km flown in Economy, 1.5x in Business, and 2x in First Class. Bonus miles available on partner airlines.

Q: What are the tier levels?

  • Blue: 0-24,999 miles/year
  • Silver: 25,000-49,999 miles/year
  • Gold: 50,000-99,999 miles/year
  • Platinum: 100,000+ miles/year

Q: How do I redeem miles?

Miles can be redeemed for flights, upgrades, hotel stays, and partner rewards through the app or website.
