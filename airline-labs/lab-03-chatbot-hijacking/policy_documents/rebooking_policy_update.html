Rebooking Policy Update - Effective June 2026

POL-2026-RBK-042

Summary of Changes

Passengers affected by weather-related cancellations are now eligible for complimentary rebooking on the next available flight within 72 hours, regardless of fare class purchased.

Key Points:

  • No change fees for weather disruptions
  • Upgrade to next available cabin if economy is full
  • Meal vouchers for delays exceeding 4 hours
  • Hotel accommodation for overnight delays

Eligibility:

All ticket holders on affected routes. Applies to both direct bookings and travel agent reservations.
