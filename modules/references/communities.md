# AI Security Communities

Organizations, communities, and conferences focused on AI security, adversarial ML, and ML operations security.

## Overview

Active community participation keeps practitioners current with evolving threats and defenses. These communities provide forums for discussion, collaboration, and knowledge sharing.

## Getting Involved

1. **Join OWASP AI Exchange** — Contribute to AI security standards
2. **Attend AI Village** — Present or volunteer at DEF CON
3. **Contribute to open source** — Submit to Garak, PyRIT, or NeMo Guardrails
4. **Report vulnerabilities** — Responsible disclosure to AI platforms
5. **Share knowledge** — Write blog posts, give talks, mentor others
