# Key AI Security Research Papers

Important academic papers on adversarial ML, prompt injection, model attacks, and AI safety.

## Overview

These papers represent foundational and cutting-edge research in AI security. Understanding these works helps practitioners stay current with attack techniques and defenses.

## Adversarial Machine Learning

| Paper | Authors | Year | Key Contribution |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| Intriguing Properties of Neural Networks | Szegedy et al. | 2014 | Discovered adversarial examples |
| Towards Evaluating the Robustness of Neural Networks | Carlini & Wagner | 2017 | C&W attack, gold-standard evaluation |
| Adversarial Examples Are Not Easily Detected | Carlini & Wagner | 2017 | Defeated 10 detection methods |
| Certified Adversarial Robustness via Randomized Smoothing | Cohen et al. | 2019 | Provable robustness guarantees |
